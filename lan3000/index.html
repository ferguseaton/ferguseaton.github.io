This is a project done for university which aims to help students find royalty free music that they can use in their own projects so they do not have worry about copyright.

To view other students work, visit this link


Being a student, do you struggle to find music that you can use in literally anything without worrying about copyright? Chances are you have looked before and come up with a lot of crap.

I have hand picked some good music, background music, podcasts and the perfect study music for you to use! And the best part? It's ROYALTY FREE!!

To download any of these songs, head over to the respective websites and download from there, I will not provide direct download links to any of them.

PLEASE NOTE, the majority of 'royalty free' music on the internet requires some sort of credit to the artist.
