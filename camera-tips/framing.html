Framing your photo is very important. A well framed photo makes for a more professional looking overall shot.

When framing your shot, we follow what we call the rule of thirds.

The rule of thirds is an imaginary grid on your photo, and you'll want to line up the subject of the photo with one of the lines, so your subject will be offset slightly.

In our app, we have a simple toggle to visualise this, but the same can be applied to any app, just by imagining roughly where the lines would be.
